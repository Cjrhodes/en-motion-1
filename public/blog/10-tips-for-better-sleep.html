10 Tips for Better Sleep

Getting a good night's sleep is crucial for your overall health and well-being. Here are 10 tips to help you improve your sleep quality:

  1. Stick to a sleep schedule
  2. Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  3. Make your bedroom comfortable
  4. Limit exposure to blue light before bed
  5. Avoid caffeine and alcohol close to bedtime
  6. Exercise regularly, but not too close to bedtime
  7. Manage stress with relaxation techniques
  8. Avoid large meals before bed
  9. Get exposure to natural daylight
  10. Consider using a white noise machine or app

Implementing these tips can significantly improve your sleep quality and overall health.
